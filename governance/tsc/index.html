Servo Technical Steering Committee

The TSC is responsible for technical oversight of the Servo Project, as laid out in the charter.

Meetings

The TSC meets in public, and all minutes are published.

Meeting minutes can be found at GitHub Servo Project repository.

Subcommittees

The TSC can form subcommittees for detailed discussion of issues. Contact the TSC secretary to join a subcommittee.

Currently there are no active subcommittees.

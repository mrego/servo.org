Servo Project Fund

The purpose of the Servo Project Fund is to raise, budget and spend funds in support of the Servo Project.

The Fund is governed by a Governing Board, as set out in the Fund Charter in the Participation Agreement . The Board is composed of Members, and representation from Mozilla, and from the Technical Steering Committee.

If your orgaziation is interested iun becoming a Member, please contact us at info@servo.org , or enroll at https://enrollment.lfx.linuxfoundation.org/?project=servo .

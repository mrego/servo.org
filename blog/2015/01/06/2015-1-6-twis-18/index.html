This Week In Servo 18

(2015-01-06) What's up with Servo for the week of 31 Dec 2014 - 6 Jan 2015

This week, we merged 17 pull requests

Vacations are over, things should pick up now!

###Notable additions

  • Martin fixed a rather annoying PaintTask leak that was making it hard to use Servo’s navigation
  • Ms2ger did some pre-rustup cleanup
  • Ms2ger completed most of the Root::deref() removal
  • Shing added support for Blob.type
  • Megha fixed our inheritance enums to properly reflect the structure of the inheritance

###New contributors

  • Bharath M R
  • Jim Hoskins
  • Megha Gupta
  • Thomas Jespersen

###Meeting

Minutes

  • Accessibility: Probably not just yet, but we plan to do something similar to what e10s is doing.
  • Cookies: We have a pull request up, needs review. Also we have hardcoded Japanese poems in the source code, apparently.
  • Rust upgrade: Almost done! We’ll probably do another rust upgrade once the alpha comes out.
